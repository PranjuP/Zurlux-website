About Zurlux

A different kind of technology company.

Zurlux Technologies is a technology holding company headquartered in Pune, India. We build enterprise SaaS products from the ground up — each one targeting a specific, underserved problem in the Indian enterprise market.

We are not a consulting firm. We do not take client projects. We build products that we own, operate, and scale — starting with PatchMortem, our automated patch remediation platform for Indian BFSI.

Indian market focus

Every product is built for the specific regulatory, infrastructure, and compliance requirements of Indian enterprise — starting with RBI IT Framework 2023.

Security by design

We build security in from day one — cryptographic audit chains, zero-trust architecture, SOC 2 readiness — not as an afterthought.

Own the full stack

We own every layer — product, infrastructure, go-to-market, and support. No dependencies on third-party platforms.

Global distribution

AWS Marketplace, Microsoft Marketplace, and direct sales — our products reach customers wherever they buy software.
